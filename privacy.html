Privacy Policy

This website utilizes Google Analytics Advertising Features (Google Signals). Google Signals compiles aggregated, cross-device data and demographics (such as age, gender, and interest groups) from users who are logged into their Google Accounts and have turned on Ads Personalization.

We and third-party vendors (including Google) use first-party cookies to optimize ad delivery, measure conversion actions, and track overall user engagement.

User Opt-Out: You can control your ad experiences or fully opt out of this tracking layer at any time by modifying your active choices on Google's Ads Settings page or by installing the official Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Additionally, our submission forms are secured via Google reCAPTCHA; Google's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service apply to its automated security analyses.

1. Information We Collect and How We Use It

When you fill out a quote request or contact form on our site, we collect personal details including your name, company name, email address, and phone number. We use this data solely to respond to your inquiries, provide commercial quotes, and optimize our advertising campaigns. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

2. Data Retention and User Rights

We retain contact information only as long as necessary to fulfill business operations or comply with legal requirements. Regardless of your location, you have the right to access, correct, or request the permanent deletion of your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@adstutorials.com.

3. Regional Privacy Notices

For United States Residents: We respect state-level privacy rights. We utilize Google's Restricted Data Processing (RDP) modes to limit data usage for opted-out users and recognize automated Global Privacy Control (GPC) opt-out signals sent by your browser.

For South Korea & Singapore Residents: All personal data collected via forms is processed transparently with your explicit consent at the point of submission. We do not use hidden trackers to gather personally identifiable information beyond standard, aggregated website analytics.
